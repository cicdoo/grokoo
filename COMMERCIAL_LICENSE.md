## Why buy a commercial license?

The LGPL-3 is permissive for *using* Grokoo, but it carries obligations —
notably, if you modify Grokoo's own source files, you must make those
modifications available under the LGPL, and you must preserve its notices. The
commercial license removes the LGPL obligations and instead grants you, on
payment of the applicable fee:

- **Proprietary modification rights** — modify Grokoo and keep your changes
  private; no copyleft obligation to publish your modifications.
- **Redistribution within closed products** — bundle Grokoo (modified or not)
  inside a proprietary Odoo distribution, SaaS offering, or appliance without
  LGPL source-disclosure requirements.
- **No LGPL notice/relinking requirements** for your downstream customers.
- **Warranty & indemnification options** and a defined **support/SLA** (by tier).
- **Priority fixes and roadmap input.**

Grokoo runs on **both Odoo Community and Odoo Enterprise** editions under either
license — it depends only on Community modules (`web`, `bus`, `mail`).
